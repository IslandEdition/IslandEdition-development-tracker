# Getting in touch with us
If you have a question about this guide, gathering data about real estate development in your area or implementing this tracker in your newsroom, contact Kate Abbey-Lambertz: kate@outliermedia.org.

If you have a question about setting up your site, contact Jimmy McBroom: james.mcbroom@gmail.com.

*We are happy to share advice and some limited assistance! If you need more extensive support, email Kate to chat about how we can help bring a development tracker to your newsroom.*

If you use this template site to build a development tracker, please share it with us!

If you have feedback or suggestions on this guide or the development tracker template, you can email us; submit them via Github's [issue-tracking system](https://github.com/jmcbroom/airtable-tracker-toolkit/issues); or clone this project [on Github](https://github.com/jmcbroom/airtable-tracker-toolkit), edit it and submit it back to us via a [pull request](https://help.github.com/en/articles/about-pull-requests).
